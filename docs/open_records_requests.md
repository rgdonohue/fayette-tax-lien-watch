# Open Records Requests

Draft requests to file under Kentucky Open Records Act (KRS 61.870-61.884). File these on Day 1; typical response window is 5 business days for initial response, though actual production may take longer.

---

## Request 1: Fayette County Clerk — Delinquent Tax Filings

**To**: Fayette County Clerk's Office

**Subject**: Open Records Request — Delinquent Tax Certificate Filings

**Body**:

Pursuant to the Kentucky Open Records Act (KRS 61.870 et seq.), I am requesting copies of the following public records in electronic, machine-readable format (CSV, Excel, or database extract preferred):

All instruments recorded in Fayette County from January 1, 2010 through the present date for the following instrument types:
- Certificate of Delinquency Assignment (CERT DEL ASSIGN)
- Reassignment of Certificate of Delinquency (REASSIGN CERTIFICATE OF DEL)
- Delinquent Tax Release (DEL TAX RELEASE)
- Third Party Tax Release (3RD PTY TAX REL)
- In House Release (IN HOUSE REL)

For each instrument, I am requesting the following fields to the extent they are maintained in your records system:
- Instrument number
- Instrument type
- Recording date
- Grantor name(s)
- Grantee name(s)
- Property address
- Parcel ID / map ID (if recorded)
- Tax bill number (if recorded)
- Tax year (if recorded)
- Amount (if recorded)

If the volume of responsive records makes electronic export impractical, I am willing to discuss narrowing the date range or accepting the records in batches.

## Request 2: Kentucky Department of Revenue — County Delinquency Lists

**To**: Kentucky Department of Revenue, Office of Property Valuation

**Subject**: Open Records Request — Fayette County Certificate of Delinquency Lists

**Body**:

Pursuant to the Kentucky Open Records Act (KRS 61.870 et seq.), I am requesting copies of the annual Fayette County certificate-of-delinquency lists submitted by the Fayette County Clerk under KRS 134.131, for all available years from 2010 through 2025.

I understand these lists contain the property owner name, property address, and parcel/lot number for each delinquent tax bill, and that the Department publishes consolidated county lists.

I am requesting these records in electronic, machine-readable format (CSV, Excel, or database extract preferred).

## Request 3: Fayette County Clerk — Certificates Excluded from Sale

**To**: Fayette County Clerk's Office

**Subject**: Open Records Request — Certificates Excluded from Third-Party Sale

**Body**:

Pursuant to the Kentucky Open Records Act (KRS 61.870 et seq.), I am requesting:

1. Any lists, logs, or records of certificates of delinquency that were excluded from third-party sale in Fayette County for tax years 2016 through 2025, including the reason for exclusion if recorded.

2. Any available records or logs related to delinquency notices mailed to property owners under KRS 134.128, including counts of notices sent and returned as undeliverable, for tax years 2016 through 2025.

I am requesting these records in electronic format where available.
